## General Information
- **Team Members:** Sapnil Basnet, Surendra Khatri, Sachin Pandey, Misan Parajuli  
- **Problem it solves:** Helps students and health-conscious individuals track calories, sleep, and overall wellness in one convenient place.  
- **Purpose:** Encourage healthier habits by providing users with insights into their daily health patterns.  
- **Why we undertook it:** As students, we face challenges balancing studies with personal well-being. This project is our solution to promote fitness and health through technology.

## Features
- 📊 **Health Data Input** – Users can enter their basic health data (age, weight, activity).  
- 🔥 **Calorie Tracking** – Tracks calories consumed and burned daily.  
- 😴 **Sleep Tracking** – Records and visualizes sleep duration.  
- 📈 **Dashboard Results** – Displays user-friendly graphs and insights about progress.
